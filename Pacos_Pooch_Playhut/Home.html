Paco's Pooch Playhut is an animal shelter. We aim to make sure every family member of ours are able to find suitable homes for themselves and be with happy loving families.

Our Success Story!

Wakka is a very active, adventurous, friendly dog. hes been with us for a very short while until he met Peyton. Our friend Wakka met his new owner Peyton here at the Paco's Pooch Playhut. They like to go on long walks and hike together through the local paths near our state park.

Paco our mascot!

This is our mascot Paco. He works with all the pets very well and befriends them! He was rescued from a a rough life of being neglected before but now he leads the shelter with his confidence and guidance as the pack leader!
